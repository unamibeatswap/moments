## üí∞ **SPONSORSHIP STRATEGY OVERVIEW**

### **Revenue Model Philosophy**
The Unami Foundation Moments platform operates on a **sustainable sponsorship model** that balances community benefit with sponsor value, ensuring ethical partnerships that enhance rather than exploit community engagement.

### **Sponsorship Principles**
1. **Community First**: All sponsored content must provide genuine value to community members
2. **Transparency**: Clear labeling and disclosure of all sponsored content
3. **Ethical Alignment**: Sponsors must align with Unami Foundation values
4. **Measurable Impact**: ROI tracking for sponsors, impact measurement for community
5. **Long-term Partnerships**: Focus on sustainable relationships over one-time transactions

- **Market Analysis**: Identify potential sponsors by sector

- **Decision Maker Identification**: Find key contacts and influencers

- **Pilot Campaign Proposal**: Low-risk trial opportunity

#### **Phase 3: Proposal Development (Week 4-5)**

- **Terms Negotiation**: Pricing, duration, and deliverables
- **Contract Finalization**: Legal review and agreement signing

### **Ethical Sponsorship Guidelines**

#### **Content Standards**
- **Community Value**: All sponsored content must benefit community members
- **Truthful Advertising**: Accurate and honest sponsor messaging
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respectful and appropriate content

- **Values Alignment**: Mission and ethics compatibility assessment
- **Reputation Review**: Public perception and controversy analysis
- **Financial Stability**: Sponsor viability and payment capability
- **Legal Compliance**: Regulatory adherence and good standing
- **Community Impact**: Positive contribution potential evaluation

### **Disclosure Requirements**

#### **Sponsored Content Labeling**
- **Clear Identification**: "Sponsored by" or "Brought to you by" labels
- **Prominent Placement**: Visible and unambiguous positioning
- **Consistent Format**: Standardized disclosure across all content
- **Multi-language Support**: Appropriate language for audience
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Adherence to advertising standards

#### **Financial Transparency**
- **Revenue Reporting**: Regular financial disclosure to stakeholders
- **Sponsor Investment**: Clear documentation of sponsor contributions
- **Community Benefit**: Demonstration of community value creation
- **Audit Compliance**: Support for financial reviews and assessments
- **Conflict of Interest**: Declaration and management procedures

### **Legal Framework**

#### **Contract Requirements**
- **Service Level Agreements**: Performance guarantees and standards
- **Intellectual Property**: Rights and usage permissions
- **Liability Protection**: Risk allocation and insurance coverage
- **Termination Clauses**: Exit procedures and conditions

#### **Regulatory Compliance**
- **Advertising Standards**: Industry regulation adherence
- **Data Protection**: Privacy and security requirements
