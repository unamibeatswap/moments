## üë• **ADMIN ROLE DEFINITIONS**

### **Superadmin (Unami Foundation Only)**
**Responsibilities:**
- Overall platform governance and strategy
- Partner onboarding and role assignment
- Financial oversight and sponsor management
- System configuration and security
- Crisis management and escalation
- Legal compliance and risk management

**Access Level:** Full system access
**Typical Users:** Unami Foundation executives, platform managers
**Account Limit:** 3-5 accounts maximum

### **Content Admin (Partner Organizations)**
**Responsibilities:**
- Create and manage Moments for their organization
- Manage sponsored content and campaigns
- Oversee regional content strategy
- Train and supervise Moderators
- Analyze content performance
- Coordinate with other partners

**Access Level:** Content creation, campaign management, analytics
**Typical Users:** Partner organization directors, communications managers
**Account Limit:** 2-3 per partner organization

### **Moderator (Community Managers)**
**Responsibilities:**
- Review and approve content before publication
- Monitor community interactions and feedback
- Handle content flags and escalations
- Support community members
- Assist with content creation
- Report to Content Admins

**Access Level:** Content review, community management, basic analytics
**Typical Users:** Community managers, program coordinators
**Account Limit:** 5-10 per partner organization

### **Viewer (Stakeholders)**
**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor content performance and impact
- Generate reports for stakeholders
- Provide feedback on content strategy
- Support fundraising and grant applications
- Observe platform operations

**Access Level:** Read-only analytics, report generation
**Typical Users:** Board members, donors, government liaisons
**Account Limit:** Unlimited (with approval)

## üìã **PARTNER ONBOARDING PROCESS**

### **Phase 1: Application & Assessment (Week 1)**

#### **Application Requirements**
1. **Organization Profile**
   - Legal registration documents
   - Mission and vision statements
   - Current programs and beneficiaries
   - Geographic areas of operation
   - Target demographics

2. **Capacity Assessment**
   - Staff size and structure
   - Communication capabilities
   - Technology infrastructure
   - Content creation experience
   - Community reach and engagement

3. **Alignment Evaluation**
   - Mission alignment with Unami Foundation
   - Commitment to community development
   - Ethical standards and practices
   - Transparency and accountability
   - Collaborative approach

#### **Assessment Criteria**
- **Mission Alignment**: 25 points
- **Community Impact**: 25 points
- **Organizational Capacity**: 20 points
- **Technical Readiness**: 15 points
- **Collaborative Potential**: 15 points

**Minimum Score for Approval**: 70/100

### **Phase 2: Agreement & Setup (Week 2)**

#### **Partnership Agreement**
- **Roles and responsibilities** definition
- **Content guidelines** and standards
- **Data sharing** and privacy terms
- **Intellectual property** rights
- **Termination conditions** and procedures

#### **Technical Setup**
- **Admin account creation** with appropriate role
- **Organization profile** setup in system
- **Regional and category** preferences configuration
- **Branding elements** upload (logo, colors)
- **Contact information** and escalation paths

#### **Initial Training Schedule**
- **Platform overview** and navigation
- **Content creation** best practices
- **Moderation workflows** and guidelines
- **Analytics interpretation** and reporting
- **Compliance requirements** and procedures

### **Phase 3: Training & Certification (Week 3-4)**

#### **Training Modules**

**Module 1: Platform Fundamentals (2 hours)**
- Platform mission and community impact
- User interface navigation and features
- Role-based access and permissions
- Basic content creation workflow
- Community guidelines and standards

**Module 2: Content Creation Excellence (3 hours)**
- Writing effective Moments
- Regional and cultural sensitivity
- Media integration and optimization
- Scheduling and timing strategies
- Engagement optimization techniques

**Module 3: Moderation & Community Management (2 hours)**
- Content review processes
- Flag handling and escalation
- Community interaction best practices
- Crisis communication protocols
- Reporting and documentation

**Module 4: Analytics & Impact Measurement (2 hours)**
- Understanding platform metrics
- Content performance analysis
- Community engagement tracking
- Report generation and interpretation
- Data-driven content optimization

**Module 5: Compliance & Ethics (1 hour)**
- Data protection and privacy
- WhatsApp Business API compliance
- Sponsor content guidelines
- Legal and ethical considerations
- Incident reporting procedures

#### **Certification Requirements**
- **Attendance**: 90% of training sessions
- **Assessment**: 80% score on knowledge test
- **Practical Exercise**: Create and publish sample content
- **Peer Review**: Feedback from existing partners
- **Commitment**: Signed learning agreement

### **Phase 4: Supervised Practice (Week 5-8)**

#### **Mentorship Program**
- **Assigned Mentor**: Experienced partner organization
- **Weekly Check-ins**: Progress review and support
- **Content Review**: All content reviewed before publication
- **Performance Monitoring**: Analytics tracking and feedback
- **Issue Resolution**: Rapid support for challenges

#### **Gradual Access Increase**
- **Week 5**: Content creation with mentor approval
- **Week 6**: Independent content creation with review
- **Week 7**: Moderation responsibilities (if applicable)
- **Week 8**: Full access with ongoing support

### **Phase 5: Full Partnership (Month 3+)**

#### **Independent Operations**
- **Full platform access** according to assigned role
- **Independent content creation** and management
- **Collaborative opportunities** with other partners
- **Performance accountability** and regular reviews
- **Continuous improvement** and skill development

### **Content Creation Workflow**

#### **Step 1: Content Planning**
- **Audience Analysis**: Identify target demographics
- **Regional Relevance**: Ensure local context and language
- **Category Selection**: Choose appropriate content category
- **Timing Strategy**: Optimize for audience engagement
- **Resource Requirements**: Identify needed media and links

#### **Step 2: Content Development**
- **Title Creation**: Compelling and clear headlines
- **Content Writing**: Engaging and informative text
- **Media Integration**: Images, videos, or documents
- **Link Addition**: Relevant external resources
- **Sponsor Integration**: If applicable, sponsor branding

#### **Step 3: Review & Approval**
- **Self-Review**: Content creator checks quality
- **Peer Review**: Colleague or mentor feedback
- **Compliance Check**: Automated system screening
- **Final Approval**: Authorized admin approval
- **Scheduling**: Set publication date and time

#### **Step 4: Publication & Monitoring**
- **Broadcast Execution**: Automated system delivery
- **Performance Tracking**: Real-time analytics monitoring
- **Community Response**: Engagement and feedback tracking
- **Issue Resolution**: Handle any problems or complaints
- **Impact Assessment**: Measure community benefit

### **Analytics & Reporting**

- **Delivery Rate**: Percentage of successful message deliveries

- **Regional Distribution**: Geographic spread of audience
- **Category Performance**: Popular content types

#### **Partner Performance Metrics**
- **Content Volume**: Number of moments created
- **Quality Scores**: Community feedback and ratings

- **Subscriber Growth**: New community member acquisition
- **Retention Rates**: Long-term engagement maintenance
- **Geographic Reach**: Coverage across target areas
- **Demographic Diversity**: Inclusive community building
