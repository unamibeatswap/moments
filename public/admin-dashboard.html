‚ú® Campaign Best Practices

üéØ Focus on Community Value

  • Provide genuine value to community members
  • Share local opportunities and resources
  • Promote community engagement and participation
  • Support local initiatives and events

üìù Content Guidelines

  • Keep messages clear, concise, and actionable
  • Use respectful, inclusive language
  • Include relevant location and timing information
  • Provide contact details for follow-up

üîÑ Frequency & Timing

  • Limit campaigns to 2-3 per week maximum
  • Send during appropriate hours (9 AM - 6 PM)
  • Avoid sending on Sundays and public holidays
  • Space campaigns at least 24 hours apart

üìä Compliance Monitoring

  • Always review the compliance check before sending
  • Get approval for medium/high-risk content
  • Monitor campaign performance and feedback
  • Report any delivery issues immediately
Remember: Our community trusts us to provide valuable, relevant content. Every campaign should enhance their experience and provide genuine value.
